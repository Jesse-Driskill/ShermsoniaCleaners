Care notes

A few helpful counter reminders.

Check pockets

Please remove pens, receipts, keys, and valuables before drop-off. It protects your clothes and the machines.

Point out stains

Tell the counter about stains, delicate fabrics, or special instructions so the team can choose the right care.

Ask about timing

Turnaround can vary by service, item type, and workload. Call ahead for rush needs or large orders.
